Welcome to

Sacred Heart Catholic Church

Dear Sacred Heart Community,

Greetings! Whether you are visiting us for the first time, new to the area, exploring the Catholic faith, returning to your spiritual journey, or already an active member of our parish, we warmly welcome you to Sacred Heart Catholic Church and School. We are delighted that you’ve found your way to our website, and we are here to support you in any way we can.

Sacred Heart Catholic Church, located on the west side of Jacksonville, is a close-knit community enriched by cultural diversity, united in faith and worship. We strive to live out our faith through acts of love and service, offering outreach programs that help those in need. Our parish offers a variety of opportunities to deepen your faith, including Religious Education for children, Youth Ministry, and Adult Faith Formation. We encourage everyone to contribute to the life of our parish by engaging in stewardship, pastoral care, and social ministries. Our Parish School, guided by the Mercedarian Sisters of the Blessed Sacrament and our dedicated teachers, provides outstanding academic and spiritual education for our youth.

If you’re new to the area or searching for a spiritual home, I personally invite you to join us for the Eucharist and experience our community. The parish staff and I are here to assist you in any way we can. Should you have any questions, please feel free to reach out to us by phone or email. For updates on parish events, please check our bulletin, Facebook page, Instagram, or myParish App.

“May the Lord bless you and protect you; may the Lord smile on you and be gracious to you; may the Lord show you his favor and give you peace.”

(Numbers 6:24-26).

In Christ & Mary Immaculate,

Fr. Saju Mathew CMI
