Igniting Young Hearts

DYNAMIC YOUTH PROGRAMS FOR ALL AGES

As a Eucharist-centered movement within the Roman Catholic Church, Life Teen leads teenagers (high school students) and their families into a deeper relationship with Jesus Christ and His Church. Life Teen has a variety of activities including social, service and spiritual. We meet on Sundays from 6:00pm-8:00pm during the school year in the Family Life Center

Edge is our Catholic middle school youth ministry community that provides a safe fun place for youth to find solid Catholic friendships, get answers to their questions about faith, and, most importantly, experience Jesus in a profound and personal way. EDGE meets every other Wednesday from 7:30pm-9:00pm during the school year in the Parish Center.
